About Us

Welcome to Vitality Dental Centre, your premier destination for exceptional dental care in Eldoret, Kenya. At Vitality Dental Centre, we are dedicated to providing top-notch dental services to our patients, ensuring their oral health and well-being are always our top priority.

Our team of experienced and skilled dental professionals is committed to delivering personalized care and treatment tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our patients based on trust, respect, and open communication.

At Vitality Dental Centre, we offer a comprehensive range of dental services, including preventive care, restorative dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, and specialized treatments. Whether you need a routine check-up, teeth whitening, or dental implants, our dedicated team is here to help you achieve a healthy and beautiful smile.

Conveniently located in Eldoret, Kenya, our modern and state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest dental technology and amenities to ensure a comfortable and stress-free experience for our patients. We strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where patients feel relaxed and at ease throughout their visit.

At Vitality Dental Centre, we are passionate about helping our patients achieve optimal oral health and confidence in their smiles. We look forward to welcoming you to our practice and providing you with the highest quality dental care you deserve.

Our Services

Preventive Dentistry

Our preventive dentistry services are designed to help patients maintain optimal oral health and prevent dental problems before they arise. We offer routine dental check-ups, professional cleanings, dental sealants, and fluoride treatments to keep your smile healthy and bright.

Restorative Dentistry

If you're experiencing dental issues such as cavities, tooth decay, or missing teeth, our restorative dentistry services can help restore your smile and oral function. From fillings and crowns to bridges and dental implants, we offer comprehensive solutions to repair and replace damaged or missing teeth.

Aesthetic Dentistry

Enhance the appearance of your smile with our aesthetic dentistry services. Whether you're looking to whiten your teeth, straighten misaligned teeth, or improve the shape and size of your teeth, we offer a range of cosmetic treatments, including teeth whitening, veneers, and clear aligner therapy.

Orthodontics

Straighten your smile and correct bite issues with our orthodontic treatments. Our experienced orthodontists offer traditional braces, clear aligners, and other orthodontic appliances to help patients achieve a straighter and healthier smile.

Oral Surgery

For patients requiring surgical interventions, we provide a range of oral surgery procedures, including tooth extractions, wisdom teeth removal, dental implants placement, and bone grafting. Our skilled oral surgeons are committed to ensuring patient comfort and safety throughout the surgical process.

Emergency Dental Care

Accidents and dental emergencies can happen when least expected. That's why we offer emergency dental care services to provide prompt treatment and relief for patients experiencing dental pain, trauma, or other urgent dental issues. Contact us immediately if you require emergency dental care.
